About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is a challenging survival game where you face endless waves of monsters.

Each wave of monsters is stronger than the last, and you need to upgrade your character, acquire new skills and weapons to handle increasingly difficult challenges. The game offers multiple character choices, each with unique skills and attributes. Upgrade by defeating monsters and gaining experience points, then choose to strengthen your attack, defense, or special abilities. Monster Survivors is easy to pick up but takes time and patience to master all strategies and techniques. How long can you survive in the onslaught of monsters?
