Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is a challenging survival game where you face endless waves of monsters. Each wave is stronger than the previous one, requiring you to constantly upgrade your character, acquire new skills and weapons to meet the increasing difficulty. The game offers various character choices, each with unique abilities and attributes. Gain experience by defeating monsters to level up and choose to enhance your attack power, defense, or special abilities. Monster Survivors is easy to pick up but mastering all the strategies and techniques requires time and patience. How long can you survive the monster siege?
